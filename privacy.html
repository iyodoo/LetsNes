Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application LetsNES ("Application") for mobile devices.

1. What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information: The Application does not require any user registration, personal accounts, or profile creations. If you contact us for customer support, we may retain your email address solely for the purpose of assisting you with your inquiry.

Automatically Collected Information & Third-Party Services:

  • In-App Advertisements & In-App Purchases: This Application integrates third-party Advertising SDKs and In-App Purchase modules to provide ads and manage premium features. These third-party services may automatically collect certain non-personal data, such as your Device Identifier (Device ID), IP address, and Advertising/Usage Data, strictly for advertising attribution, frequency capping, analytics, and preventing transaction fraud.
  • Online Multiplayer Feature: The Application provides remote multiplayer functionalities via a room code mechanism. Room connections and multi-user data synchronization are processed dynamically to facilitate the gameplay connection. No in-game communications or voice data are ever recorded, monitored, or stored on our infrastructure.

2. Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise real-time location information of your mobile device. However, third-party advertising networks may utilize coarse location data (such as country or city level) to deliver relevant regional advertisements.

3. Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Data is only accessed by trusted third-party service providers (such as advertising networks and payment processors) to the extent necessary to maintain app functionalities, as described in Section 1.

4. Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

Since we do not collect or store any personal user data on our own servers, there is no personal user data retained on our infrastructure. Third-party advertising and analytics platforms handle data retention in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

5. Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us immediately.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
