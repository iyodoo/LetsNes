Let's NES

A Classic Emulator with Multiplayer Room Voice Chat

Features

Welcome to Let's NES, the ultimate tool to relive your favorite classic games with friends.

  • Local & Online Multiplayer: Easily create or join a room using a simple room ID to play with friends.
  • Real-time Voice Chat: Connect with your teammates instantly using built-in high-quality room voice chat.
  • Pure Experience: Standard version is supported by Google ads. Go Premium via a one-time In-App Purchase to remove all ads forever and unlock exclusive room controls.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user data. We value your privacy and aim to be fully transparent.

1. Data Collection & Usage

  • In-App Purchases When you purchase the "Remove Ads" or "Premium features", the transaction is securely processed by the Google Play Billing API. We collect your Purchase History solely to verify your premium status and unlock functionalities.
  • Voice Stream For the multiplayer voice chat feature, your mic audio is processed ephemerally in real-time. We do NOT record, save, or store your voice data on any server.

2. Account and Data Deletion

Let's NES does NOT require or maintain any user accounts. All game saves and configurations are stored locally on your device.

Since we do not host user data on our servers, you can completely erase all local data and caches at any time by simply clearing the app data or uninstalling the application from your device settings.

3. Data Security

All data transmitted through our app (including secure billing information and real-time voice streams) is fully encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.
